Made a WatermelonBoy and tested it out in public [OP]

  • Watermelon
  • Gameboy
  • Buttons and wires
  • Knife

Instructions

Cut circles into the Watermelon using the knife. Make sure the circles are cut into places confenient for your fingers to reach when you play. Cut a hole for the screen and a hole in the back to slide your Gameboy in. Dismantle your Gameboy and use Buttons and wires to attach it all to the watermelon. This kid is actually a genius.
