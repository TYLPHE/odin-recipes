Whose bright idea was this

Items Required

  • Phone Charger
  • Paper clip
  • Electrical Socket
  • USB phone cable

Instructions

Bend the paper clip in a way where the two ends create hooks (these will hook onto the phone charger's prongs). Bend the middle of the paper clip for the USB phone cable to hang. Then hook the paper clip onto the two prongs of the phone charger. Plug the paper clip and phone charger into the electrical socket to make the hook sturdy. You will now have organized cables when you charge your phone!
